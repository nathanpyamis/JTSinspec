Conventional and Advanced NDT Services

Explore our full range of certified inspection solutions, tailored to PNG conditions.

  • Ultrasonic Testing (UT) — detects internal flaws and wall thickness. Standards: AS 2207, ASME V, API 5UE
  • Magnetic Particle Testing (MT/MPT) — surface & near-surface defects. Standards: AS 1171, ASME V, ASTM E709
  • Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT/LPT) — surface-breaking defects. Standards: AS 2062, ASME V, ASTM E165
  • Radiographic Testing (RT) — X-ray/gamma radiation for internal flaws. Standards: ASME V, API 1104, ISO 17636
  • Visual Testing (VT) — first-line inspection for welds & structures. Standards: AS 3978, ASME V, API 577

  • Eddy Current Testing (ET) — Eddy current testing detects the smallest defects in various materials that can minimize downtime and decrease inspection cost. At JTS Inspection Services, our certified technicians use specialized eddy current testing equipment to provide a quick and acurate examination of Aircraft and Engine Components, Heat Exchanger Tubing and Other Critical Engineering components Standards: AS 4544, ASTM E243, ASME V
  • Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing (PAUT) — Phased Array Ultrasonic system utilize multi-element probes, which are individually excited under computer control. By exiting each element in a controlled manner, a focus beam of Ultrasonic can be generate. Software enables the beam to be steered. Two and three dimensional views can be generated shown the sizes and locations of any flaws detected. PAUT used to detect discontinuities such as cracks or flaws and thereby determine component’s quality. Due to the possibility to control parameters such as beam angle and focus distance, this method is very inefficient regarding the defect and speed of testing. Advanced UT for 2D/3D flaw imaging in welds and complex geometries.
    Standards: ASME V, API 579, ISO 13588
