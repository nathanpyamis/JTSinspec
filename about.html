Our Story

Established in 2023, JTS Inspection Services is a 100% Papua New Guinean-owned company specializing in Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) and Asset Integrity. Operating from hubs in Madang and Port Moresby, we deliver certified inspection solutions across mining, oil & gas, marine, power, and manufacturing sectors.
