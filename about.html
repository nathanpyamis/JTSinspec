About Our Company

JTS Inspection Services is a 100% PNG-owned Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) and Asset Integrity company. We deliver certified inspection solutions across mining, oil & gas, marine, power, and manufacturing sectors.

Our Story

Established in 2023, JTS Inspection Services is a 100% Papua New Guinean-owned company specializing in Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) and Asset Integrity. Operating from hubs in Madang and Port Moresby, we deliver certified inspection solutions across mining, oil & gas, marine, power, and manufacturing sectors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide current and future customers with the highest quality advanced Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) equipment, services, and support. We take pride in short lead times, rapid delivery, and quality services that enable our clients to meet evolving job requirements. Our Team strives to provide world-class services and support after the sale to ensure the customer/client remains up and running without interruptions.

  • Reliable, Conventional, Advanced NDT Services and Asset Integrity solutions with rapid turnaround, uncompromising Quality, Safety and exceptional Customer Support.
  • All services delivered in compliance with international standards including AS, ASME, API, ISO, LEEA, and IRATA.

Our Vision

To be the trusted leader in asset integrity and NDT solutions in Papua New Guinea, recognized for innovation, professionalism, and community impact.

Our Values

  • Integrity and transparency
  • Commitment to safety
  • Excellence in service delivery
  • Empowering local communities
